IIT Delhi Thesis and Supervisor

Thesis Title: Transactional Data Analytics and Disease Profiling

Supervisor: Dr. Varun Ramamohan

Description: In this paper, we have tried to provide an efficient and cost-effective methodology as an alternative way of profiling chronic disease patterns at the zip code level, in contrast to existing methods like NFHS and IDSP. Here we have taken the data of a popular e-pharmacy chain in India, and based on this, we have established a methodology for doing this profiling. As the final output of this analysis, we have shown the top three chronic diseases prevalent at any pin code and how the pattern changes with time.

Courses in IEOR

The Industrial Engineering & Operations Research group offers a diverse range of courses spanning foundational theory, computational methods, and practical applications. This comprehensive course catalog includes undergraduate and graduate offerings in areas such as optimization, statistics, manufacturing systems, operations management, and analytics. Students can explore these courses as part of degree programs or as electives to develop expertise in specialized IEOR domains.

Publications in IEOR

The Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at IIT Delhi focuses on advancing the field of Operations Research, particularly in areas such as Optimization, Machine Learning, and Data Science. These fields are characterized by their interdisciplinary nature and diverse applications. Consequently, research at IEOR covers a wide range of topics, from fundamental mathematical questions in optimization and algorithm design to collaborative projects with industries.
By bridging theoretical foundations and practical applications, IEOR at IIT Delhi plays a pivotal role in driving interdisciplinary research and innovation, contributing significantly to both academic and industrial communities.

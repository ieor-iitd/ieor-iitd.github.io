Publications in IEOR

The Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at IIT Delhi focuses on advancing the field of Operations Research, particularly in areas such as Optimization, Machine Learning, and Data Science. These fields are characterized by their interdisciplinary nature and diverse applications. Consequently, research at IEOR covers a wide range of topics, from fundamental mathematical questions in optimization and algorithm design to collaborative projects with industries. IEOR serves as a hub, supporting various departments at IIT Delhi by addressing optimization challenges through mathematical modeling, analysis, and computational solutions. Moreover, IEOR has extensive experience in industrial collaborations, fostering innovation across sectors such as Aviation, logistics, healthcare, and more. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art HP server, enabling cutting-edge research in areas like digital twins, public systems, Health care systems and supply chain management.
By bridging theoretical foundations and practical applications, IEOR at IIT Delhi plays a pivotal role in driving interdisciplinary research and innovation, contributing significantly to both academic and industrial communities.

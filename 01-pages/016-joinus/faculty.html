Faculty Recruitment

IIT Delhi invites applications for faculty positions from distinguished professionals, including those with non-academic or industrial backgrounds. We strongly encourage individuals from SC/ST/OBC/EWS categories, as well as persons with disabilities,to apply. We are seeking top experts across a wide range of disciplines. Faculty applications are accepted throughout the year on a rolling basisâ€”there is no deadline to apply. Please note that the faculty recruitment process at IIT Delhi is multi-stage and rigorous. Applications are first screened by administrative staff to ensure they meet the minimum eligibility criteria. Eligible applications are then evaluated by the relevant academic units. Shortlisted candidates are invited for an extended interaction with the faculty members. The most outstanding candidates proceed to a final interview conducted by the Institute Selection Committee, which includes eminent international experts and a nominee of the Hon'ble Visitor. The recommendations from this committee are compiled into a compendium, which is presented to the Board of Governors for final approval. Given the comprehensive nature of the process, it may take up to six months or longer for a final decision.

Benefits & Incentives to Faculty

IIT Delhi has always motivated its faculty to excel at work and reach great heights. Salaries are at par with the top institutions of the country. Faculty members are eligible for various types of research grants. Funding is available internally from the institute, and there are many more opportunities for extramural grants. In addition, IIT Delhi has active associations with various government and corporate bodies that provide a variety of platforms and resources to help faculty members. IIT Delhi campus has spacious apartments and residential bungalows for faculty where they can live and work in peace. All varieties of utilities exist on-campus so that the faculty members can focus on teaching and research single-mindedly. The campus has modern gymnasia, food joints and marketplaces for daily needs that eliminate the urban commute. If someone wants to take a break from work and rejuvenate themselves, they simply step out to stroll the lawns and enjoy the fresh air.

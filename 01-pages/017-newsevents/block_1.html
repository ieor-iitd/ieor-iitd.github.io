Dr. Boli Chen in IIT Delhi

Dr. Boli Chen from UCL visited IIT Delhi on February 13,2025 for a talk on "Intelligent Control for Smart and Sustainable Mobility". His talk on control and decision-making challenges in connected and automated vehicles (CAVs), including, coordinated platooning and smart charging technology, was very insightful. Engaging discussions followed on the future of intelligent control systems and their impact on mobility and sustainability.
Dr. Chen also interacted with the students in the "Optimization" course. We thank him for his visit to IEOR, IIT Delhi.

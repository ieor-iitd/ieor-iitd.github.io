Large Scale Optimization summer school at IIT Delhi (June 1-6)

March 25, 2026

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi is hosting the 6th edition of Large Scale Optimization (LSO) Summer School from June 1-6, 2026.

India's premier intensive week of advanced learning in optimization covering theory, modeling, and applications with hands-on computing sessions conducted by leading faculty from IITs and IIMs. Topics include Linear Optimization, Nonlinear Optimization, Integer Optimization, and Real-world Applications.

Target audience includes students, faculty members, industry professionals and government officials.

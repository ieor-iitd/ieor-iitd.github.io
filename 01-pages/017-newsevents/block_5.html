Dr. Ankur Garg joins IIT Delhi as Adjunct Professor

September 1, 2025

IEOR Group proudly welcomes Dr. Ankur Garg as an Adjunct Professor.

With his extensive expertise and experience, Dr. Garg is set to bring valuable insights and opportunities for innovation, research, and academic growth to the institute. Dr. Garg, known for his expertise in Commercial function for airlines encompassing network planning, revenue management, ancillary & cargo, sales & distribution, marketing & PR, customer support and regulatory affairs.

His association is expected to foster strong collaborations and enrich the learning experience for students and researchers alike.

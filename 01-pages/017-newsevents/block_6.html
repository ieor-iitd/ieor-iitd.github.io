IEOR Group welcomes new batch of M.Tech and Ph.D Students

August 1, 2025

The Group of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at IIT Delhi extends a warm welcome to its new batch of M.Tech and PhD students.

The Group looks forward to seeing them embark on an exciting journey of learning and discovery. New students will have the opportunity to engage in cutting-edge research and innovative projects. They will also gain exposure to interdisciplinary fields and real-world problem-solving. IEOR provides a vibrant environment for academic growth and industry collaboration. Faculty and senior students are eager to support and guide them throughout their journey.

The Group wishes all newcomers great success in their academic and research pursuits.

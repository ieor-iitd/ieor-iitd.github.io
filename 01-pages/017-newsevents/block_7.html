"Seminar on AI-Driven Fraud Detection in Asian Health Insurance "by Mr. Sankara Prasad Kondareddy at IIT Delhi

March 27, 2025

The Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) group at IIT Delhi organized an insightful seminar featuring Mr. Sankara Prasad Kondareddy, Head of AI & Data Science at Amplify Health, Singapore. The seminar focused on "AI Solutions for Fraud, Waste, and Abuse Detection in Asian Health Insurance", addressing the growing challenges faced by the healthcare sector in detecting and preventing fraudulent claims, unnecessary procedures, and misuse of resources.

Mr. Kondareddy shared how cutting-edge artificial intelligence techniques and data-driven models are being applied to analyze vast amounts of health insurance data, identify patterns of fraud, and improve efficiency within healthcare systems. He discussed real-world case studies from Asian markets, highlighting both the technical and operational aspects of implementing AI solutions in a sensitive and highly regulated domain like health insurance.

The seminar provided students and faculty with valuable insights into the intersection of AI, data science, and healthcare management, sparking engaging discussions on the future role of technology in building more transparent and sustainable healthcare systems.

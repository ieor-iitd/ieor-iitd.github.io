Welcome to IEOR

Industrial Engineering & Operations Research (IEOR) focuses on optimizing complex systems through mathematical modeling, data analytics, and decision sciences. It integrates engineering, management, and computational techniques to improve efficiency in manufacturing, supply chains, logistics, and service industries. Our programs emphasize upon industrial engineering, operations research, artificial intelligence, optimization, and statistical modeling, equipping students with analytical and problem-solving skills for real-world challenges.
